THIS IS OUR STORY

The journey began in 2006 while attending culinary school in Florida. Before finishing school, I fell upon hardships and had to return to North Carolina. I initially started out perfecting my craft with BBQ/Soul food. We started selling BBQ and ribs while researching ways to start a trailer. My plan was to be the BBQ king.

I joined many cook offs and even became a certified judge. We found that simplifying the menu was the fastest and best option to get us up and running. So, while BBQ was my passion, making phillies and salads would be where we started with the menu.

While working to perfect my grilling skills, from 2007-2019 I worked at various restaurants. The restaurants ranged from substations to high end restaurants and finally my last official place of employment when COVID struck, a corporate/ government cafeteria. It was there that I introduced my wife and friends to the shrimp taco. They loved them so much my wife requested them daily for three months straight. We had found our star of the menu.

Of course, I put my own spin on it and adjusted where I saw others were lacking (we tried a lot of shrimp tacos). Once the pandemic was in full swing and many were turning to mobile food trucks for meals as opposed to sit down eateries, we decided it was time to officially open and have been selling tacos ever since.

Now the naming of the trailer was a little harder that expected. I wanted something that would represent me and include my name, let people know that it’s me. Long story short I decided its “me” cooking on the grill…. Hence “Gator on the Grill”.
